WELCOME TO TRACK IT

Track It is a simple and user-friendly expense tracking application designed to help users manage their daily spending. It allows users to record expenses, categorize them, and monitor their financial habits in an organized way. With Track It, users can:

  • Add and manage daily expenses
  • Categorize expenses such as Food, Travel, Bills, and Shopping
  • View and track their spending over time
  • Maintain better control over their personal finances
